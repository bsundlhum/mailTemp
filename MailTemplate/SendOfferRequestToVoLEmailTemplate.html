Dear Engagement Team and Vertical Operations Leader,

Now that the Engagement team is actively reviewing talent (e.g., shortlisting) for the below role on Talent Exchange, we would like to provide some guidance and support.

SCREENING

Please be sure to capture your screening notes in the Talent Exchange so all relevant PwC users can benefit from your feedback. You can launch a screening for a talent that is shortlisted or has shown interest by clicking on the more icon with 3 dots in the upper right hand corner of the talent card.

To learn more information about screening, please   Watch Video.

PRICING EVENT

Let a pricing event do the negotiating for you! Not only does a pricing event allow you to benefit from more competitive rates through this form of a reverse auction, it provides new levels of pricing transparency to talent. It is easy to set up.

To learn more information about Pricing Event, please   Watch Video.

If you are not able to conduct a pricing event, please be sure to get approval for an exception from your Vertical Operations Leader.
